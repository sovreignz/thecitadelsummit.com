FAQs

Who is this not for? +

The Citadel is not for those seeking a passive vacation. It's an intensive experience for individuals committed to growth, contribution, and mastering new skills. If you are not ready to be challenged and engaged, this may not be the right fit.

What's the difference between this and other men's retreats? +

While we foster deep camaraderie, The Citadel is not exclusively a men's retreat. Our focus is on tangible skill acquisition (specifically AI) and holistic development, rather than just escape or adventure. We combine deep inner work with practical, future-proof skills.

Can I come solo? +

Absolutely. Most of our attendees come solo. It's a powerful way to build new, high-caliber connections and focus entirely on your own growth without external distractions.

What if I don't have a tech background? +

No prior tech experience is necessary. Our AI curriculum is designed from the ground up to take you from foundational concepts to practical application. The only prerequisite is a strong desire to learn and apply new knowledge.
